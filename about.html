About OfferVault

OfferVault is a free career decision tool that helps users compare job offers, estimate take-home pay, understand hidden costs, and make smarter salary decisions.

Why This Tool Exists

Choosing between two job offers is not always simple. A higher salary may not always be the better option when taxes, commute costs, benefits, insurance, retirement match, and growth potential are included.

What You Can Do Here

  • Compare two job offers side by side.
  • Estimate annual and monthly take-home pay.
  • Check hidden costs like commute and insurance.
  • Adjust salary for cost of living differences.
  • Create a simple salary negotiation script.

Important Note

All results are estimates for informational purposes only. OfferVault does not provide financial, tax, or legal advice.
